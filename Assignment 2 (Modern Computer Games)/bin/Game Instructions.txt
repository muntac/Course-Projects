GAME Instructions

To toggle features press buttons 1 to 5. Each number corresponds to the features
needed for that question number on the assignment i.e. pressing 5 enables all features.

Rest of the controls are as specified in the assignment description.

The white bar at top left represents firing speed.
The orange bar below it represents the feature state from 1 to 5.

Wind direction/strength represented by the varying yellow bar on the far right.
